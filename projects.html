Castle Self Storage – Unit Table

During my internship, I had the opportunity to contribute to updates and improvements for a client-facing self-storage website, specifically styling the unit tables from the ground up. Working on a live production site introduced me to the responsibilities that come with developing for real users, where accuracy, consistency, and attention to detail are critical. I worked within an existing site structure to help implement updates and ensure the page maintained a clear layout and user-friendly experience.

This experience helped me better understand how professional development environments differ from classroom projects. I learned how to navigate and contribute to an established codebase while maintaining the design and functionality already in place. Through this process I gained valuable insight into real-world workflows and how small updates can have a meaningful impact on the usability of a client website.

Web Development II Group Project

This project was completed as part of a group web development assignment where we collaborated to design and build a functional website from the ground up. Working as part of a team required us to divide responsibilities, coordinate design decisions, and ensure that our individual contributions worked cohesively within the final product. Throughout the project I focused on implementing core web development concepts while maintaining a consistent layout and navigation structure across the site.

One of the most valuable aspects of this project was learning how collaboration influences the development process. Web Development I Group Project

This portfolio-style website was created as part of my web development class project using WordPress. The goal of the project was to design and structure a website that clearly presented information while maintaining an organized and visually engaging layout. Working with WordPress introduced me to the process of building and managing websites through a content management system rather than coding every element from scratch. I worked within the platform to structure pages, organize content, and ensure the design remained consistent across the site.

Through this project I gained experience navigating the WordPress dashboard, editing pages through the CMS interface, and understanding how themes, layouts, and content blocks work together to create a cohesive website. It also helped me see how CMS platforms are used in professional environments to effeciently manage and update websites. This experience strengthened my understanding of both traditional front-end development and CMS-based website management.
